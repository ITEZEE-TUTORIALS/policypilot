# GitLab Global Travel and Expense Policy

Source: https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/finance/expenses/

## Lodging
- Standard hotel stays are reimbursable up to $300 USD per night, including tax, for standard rooms.
- Refundable room bookings are preferred unless a non-refundable option is the only available choice.
- Hotels may still require a card on file for incidentals.
- Hotel minibar charges are not reimbursable.

## Additional hotel guidance
- Dry cleaning is allowed if the stay is longer than 5 days.
- Room service can be submitted as a meal when it is breakfast, lunch, or dinner.
- Meals should be separated from the hotel bill and categorized correctly.
- Parking should be separated and categorized as transportation.
- Movies and minibar charges are not allowed as claims.

## Meals and travel expenses
- Meals while traveling on company business are reimbursable up to $120 USD per day, or local equivalent.
- Team members must submit their own receipts.
- Tips are acceptable up to 20% when they are documented correctly.

## General guidelines
- Receipts are required for every business expense for non-US team members.
- US team members need receipts for expenses over $50 USD.
- Expense descriptions should clearly explain what was purchased and why it was needed.

## Transportation
- Parking, tolls, taxi, and rideshare charges related to GitLab travel are acceptable.
- Ground transportation has per-trip limits and may require manager approval when over policy thresholds.
