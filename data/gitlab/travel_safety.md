# GitLab Travel Safety and Security

Source: https://handbook.gitlab.com/handbook/finance/travel/

## Core principle
- Team member safety and security are a top priority.
- If travel feels unsafe because of location, health, or security concerns, GitLab asks team members to contact People Safety & Security for help.
- Team member travel is not required unless it is essential to the role.

## Trade compliance
- Company-owned devices should not be taken to US-embargoed countries or regions.
- GitLab also restricts travel with company-owned devices to Russia and Belarus due to trade control requirements.

## Emergency support
- GitLab provides 24/7 business travel assistance through International SOS.
- Support covers medical emergencies, security concerns, pre-trip planning, and claims assistance.
- Team members can use the app, phone, or email to reach assistance.

## Business travel insurance
- Business travel coverage is automatic and year-round for eligible business travel.
- The policy includes medical, security evacuation, baggage, and family coverage details.
